Procedural - Incremental game with gems

Gems spawns randomly all over the map
Different gems give different currencies
Harvesters collect the gems and brings them home as currency
Use currency for upgrade

Upgrades
 - More Harvesters
 - Faster Harvesters
 - Carry Capacity for Harvesters
 - More gems spawning
 - More gem quality per gem (bigger?)
 - Zoom out camera for more area to spawn gems
